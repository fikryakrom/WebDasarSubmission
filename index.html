Malang Raya

A region located in East Java, encompassing 3 municipality with more than 3 million people resides in the area.
Well-known as the home of notable universities in national level, as well as a tourism centre in East Java

Kab. Malang

The second largest regency in East Java, a total area of 3,530.65 km2. Stretch from Javanese middle plateu down to southern coast. Rich of potentials (agriculture, tourism).      

Kota Malang

The second most populous city in East Java, with a total population up to 843,810 people (2020 National Cencus). Home to some reputable universities in national levels.      

Kota Batu

Located 20 km to the northwest of Malang. Formerly a part of Malang Regency, but became an independent city in 2001. Well-known as the beacon of 'East Java' tourism.
